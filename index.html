Hi, I’m Markus!

I am a postdoctoral researcher in robotics with many years of R&D experience in robotics motion control, software development for manipulators, and industrial applications (both onshore and offshore).

I've worked in diverse fields such as the ship industry, where I developed electrical power systems and drives, and the Oil & Gas sector, focusing on subsea control systems.

What I enjoy the most? Hands-on work in the lab with robots and the amazing people around me, cycling, and mindset of ROS 2 and Linux!

Feel free to explore my repositories, check out my industrial projects, or learn about my current research. You can also contact me to discuss exciting robotics projects and research!
